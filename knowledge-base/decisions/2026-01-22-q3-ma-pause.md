# Decision: Q3 2026 M&A pause to address integration debt

**Status.** Accepted 2026-01-22. Pause begins Q3 2026.

## Context

Pawline acquired 14 clinics in 2025, the most aggressive year of the rollup. By Q4 2025 the integration team was running behind on T+90 and T+180 milestones across the 2025 cohort. [[acquired-clinic-revenue-dip-month-3]] showed the typical month-3 revenue dip was running 8% — concerning when concentrated across a large cohort. The 2025 cohort's average ADR variance ([[adr-variance-3x-top-vs-bottom-quartile]]) signaled clinical-quality unevenness post-close.

Larkspur (per [[david-lansky]]) preferred continued deployment. Sarah's view: integration debt compounds, and the rollup's reputation as a quality consolidator is at risk if we keep pushing volume.

## Decision

Pause new acquisition closings for Q3 2026 (July-September). Allow LOI activity to continue with extended diligence timelines. Use Q3 to:

- Close T+90 and T+180 milestones for the 2025 cohort
- Fix the West-region ROD gap ([[2025-11-10-medical-director-dyad-rollout]])
- Complete 2026 H1 PIMS migrations on schedule
- Re-evaluate the [[acquisition-criteria]] thresholds in light of [[competitor-acquisition-multiples-rising]]

## Alternatives considered

- **No pause**: rejected. Compounding integration debt was already showing clinical-quality early-warning signs.
- **Full-year 2026 pause**: too aggressive. Would have lost good targets to competitors and hurt the platform's deployment posture with Larkspur.
- **Q4 2026 pause**: weaker — Q3 is operationally the right time given vacation cadence and back-half hiring.
- **Selective pause (smaller deals only, large deals proceed)**: rejected. Integration capacity is the constraint, not deal size.

## Consequences

- **Deal flow loss**: ~3-5 targets that would have closed in Q3 will either slip to Q4 or be lost to competitors
- **Larkspur friction**: visible disappointment from David Lansky; mitigated through documented integration-debt evidence
- **Internal capacity recovery**: integration team and regional dyads can reset
- **Quality protection**: signals to the network and to the market that Pawline values integration quality over deployment pace
- **Texas pipeline pivot**: the pause overlaps with [[texas-acquisition-pipeline-thinning]] reality — no high-quality TX targets queued anyway

## Resumption criteria

- 2025-cohort T+180 milestones >85% complete
- West-region ROD seat filled
- 2026 H1 PIMS migration plan on track
