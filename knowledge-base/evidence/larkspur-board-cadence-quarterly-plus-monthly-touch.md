# Larkspur board cadence — quarterly + monthly touch

The Larkspur-Pawline cadence operating norm: formal quarterly board meeting, plus a monthly informal partner touch ([[tom-brennan]] with [[david-lansky]]) and a quarterly informal CEO call ([[dr-sarah-okafor]] with David).
