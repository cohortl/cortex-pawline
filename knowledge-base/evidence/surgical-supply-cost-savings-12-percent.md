# Surgical supply cost savings 12%

Q4 2024 procurement audit of surgical supplies across 65 clinics documented 8-20% pricing variance for identical SKUs (sutures, drapes, gloves, sutures, anesthesia disposables). Modeled negotiated central-buying scenario at then-current network volume yielded 11-13% savings — a $2.5-3M annualized opportunity. Realized post-implementation savings 2025: 12.1% on the surgical category, $2.7M annualized — close to the modeled estimate. Note: the 12% was net of one-time switching costs (vendor transitions, NetSuite portal build) which were ~$300K. This is the headline evidence for [[2025-04-15-centralize-surgical-procurement]] and the precedent for further centralization considerations.
