# Urgent care → ER conversion 30%

At the three Tier A flagship clinics with both urgent-care and ER footprint (San Diego, Phoenix, Houston), ~30% of urgent-care presentations escalate to an ER referral on the same day or within 48 hours. Methodology: Cornerstone visit-chain analysis Q4 2025-Q1 2026, n≈4,200 UC visits. Confidence is medium rather than high because UC and ER classification at non-flagship clinics is less consistent — the 30% is potentially over- or under-stated by 5 percentage points depending on classification rigor. The baseline finding is the strongest single argument for the in-network ER capture case in [[2026-03-30-emergency-clinic-line-of-service]] — currently most of those referrals leave the Pawline network.
