# Lab vendor consolidation savings

The 2024 consolidation from a 3-vendor outside-lab mix (Antech, Idexx, regional independents) to Antech-primary + Idexx-secondary saved $1.8M annualized. Drivers: (1) volume-based pricing renegotiation with Antech at the new consolidated volume; (2) elimination of administrative overhead from per-clinic vendor accounts; (3) preferential pricing on the most-frequently-ordered tests. Trade-off: small clinic-level resistance, especially in clinics where Idexx had longstanding relationships with the local-lab account managers — addressed by keeping Idexx as secondary. This finding was the precedent that Marcus Kim cited when proposing the surgical-procurement centralization that became [[2025-04-15-centralize-surgical-procurement]]: "lab consolidation worked, surgical supplies will work, drugs are next."
