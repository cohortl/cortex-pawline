# PIMS data quality — Cornerstone 3x cleaner than Avimark

Pre-standardization data audit (Q3 2024) compared Cornerstone vs Avimark clinics across six data-quality metrics: patient-record completeness, vaccine-history accuracy, lab-result linkage, controlled-substance log integrity, charge-line completeness, and visit-chain referencability. Cornerstone was 2.8-3.4x better across the dimensions; geometric mean ~3.0x. The gap is structural: Cornerstone enforces required fields in workflow while Avimark allows free-text shortcuts that erode over time. This evidence was the technical anchor for the [[2024-09-12-cornerstone-as-standard-pims]] standardization decision; the operational anchor was workflow / lab integration / controlled-substance handling.
