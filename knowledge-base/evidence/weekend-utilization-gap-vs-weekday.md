# Weekend utilization gap vs weekday

Across all 80 clinics, weekend appointment utilization is 22 percentage points below weekday: weekday slots fill 84%, weekend slots 62%. Saturday is meaningfully better than Sunday (Saturday fills ~75%, Sunday ~50%). The pattern is consistent across regions, slightly worse in Mountain than West/Southwest. Implications: (1) weekend visit demand exists but isn't capturing — likely a function of inconsistent weekend appointment availability across the network; (2) urgent-care positioning could capture weekend demand if expanded — connects to [[urgent-care-vs-er-vs-gp]] strategy; (3) DVM scheduling preferences (most DVMs avoid Sundays) reinforces the pattern but isn't the only driver. Has not yet driven a formal decision; flagged as a 2026-2027 operational opportunity.
