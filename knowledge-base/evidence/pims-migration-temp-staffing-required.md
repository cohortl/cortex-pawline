# PIMS migration — 2 weeks of temp staffing required

Across 12 completed migrations 2024-2025, every clinic required 2 weeks of supplementary temp staffing in the cutover stabilization period — typically a floating relief technician + a part-time training-support lead. Without temp staffing, three clinics in early-2024 attempted to absorb the migration with existing headcount and saw 2-3x normal technician overtime, two staff resignations, and patient-care delays. Standardized practice since Q3 2024: temp staffing is booked at T-2 weeks, runs through T+2 weeks. Cost per migration: ~$18-25K, included in the [[cornerstone-migration-15-to-25k-per-clinic]] band. This finding is essentially a sub-component of migration cost economics but documented separately because acquired-clinic owners often question why the temp staffing line is there.
