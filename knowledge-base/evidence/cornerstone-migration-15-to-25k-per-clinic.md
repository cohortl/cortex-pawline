# Cornerstone migration cost band: $15-25K per clinic

Across 12 completed migrations 2024-2025, direct costs landed in a $15-25K per clinic band. Components: Cornerstone implementation services ($6-9K), Pawline IT staffing for cutover weekend ($4-7K), staff training time ($3-6K including DVM and tech CE), legacy data extraction work ($2-3K). Excludes operational productivity drag during the 4-8 week stabilization period — that's a separate line, harder to quantify but typically 5-12% of monthly revenue for stabilization weeks. Cost band is consistent across regions; smaller variance than initially expected.
