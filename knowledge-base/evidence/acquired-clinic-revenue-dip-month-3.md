# Acquired clinic revenue dip — month 3

Across 22 acquired clinics 2024-2025, the typical revenue trajectory dips ~8% from baseline around month 3 post-close, recovering to or above baseline by month 6. Drivers (qualitative): (1) seller-DVM panel transition friction — long-tenured clients adjust to new ownership; (2) early integration friction — vendor changes, schedule disruptions; (3) coding alignment — protocol-aligned coding sometimes lowers RPV initially before recovering. The dip is more pronounced in Tier B/C clinics (10-12%) than Tier A (4-6%). Watching the pattern in real-time is what triggered the cohort-level concern that led to [[2026-01-22-q3-ma-pause]].
