# Equity program take-up rate — 78%

78% of eligible DVMs accepted equity grants in 2025 (118 of 152 eligible DVMs). The 22% who declined cited: cash preference (39%), tax/timing concerns (28%), near-term retirement plans (18%), skepticism about hold-period exit timing (10%), other (5%). The take-up rate is comparable to other PE-backed multi-site healthcare platforms in the 70-85% band; not exceptional but solid. The 10% citing exit-timing skepticism is the most actionable category — addressable through better communication about Larkspur's hold horizon ([[2025-01-20-larkspur-second-round]]). Take-up rate among Practice Managers (eligible at 24-month tenure) is materially lower (~58%); cash preference dominates there.
