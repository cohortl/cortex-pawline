# Client acquisition cost rising Q1 2026

Paid CAC (Salesforce + Google + Meta combined) for Q1 2026 was $128 per new client, up 18% from Q4 2025 ($108) and up 32% from Q1 2025 ($97). Drivers (qualitative + quantitative): (1) Google Ads CPC inflation in the vet-services category, particularly in California metros; (2) competitive pressure from larger consolidators bidding on the same keywords; (3) seasonal effect (Q1 is typically slightly elevated, but not by 18 ppt). Implication: organic and partnership-based acquisition channels become disproportionately valuable. Reinforces the case for [[2026-04-12-fixed-fee-pricing-pilot]] (subscription pricing as a retention/acquisition lever) and [[2026-04-29-pet-insurance-partnership]] (insurance partnership as a referral channel).
