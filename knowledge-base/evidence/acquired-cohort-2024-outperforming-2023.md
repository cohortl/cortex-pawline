# Acquired-cohort 2024 outperforming 2023 at T+12

The 2024 acquisition cohort (10 clinics) is posting 12% higher production at T+12 months than the 2023 cohort (8 clinics) at the same milestone. Drivers identified: (1) more disciplined [[acquisition-criteria]] application starting Q3 2023 reduced "stretch" deals; (2) [[acquisition-integration-playbook]] T+30/T+90/T+180 framework codified late 2023, applied consistently to 2024 cohort; (3) [[medical-director-dyad]] available in 2024 in West for those clinics. Evidence basis: revenue per clinic, normalized for tier, region, and PIMS migration status.
