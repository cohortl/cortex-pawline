# NPS correlates with medical-director engagement

Clinics where the RMD or local Medical Director conducts monthly chart rounds + walks the floor monthly post +18 NPS points compared to clinics where this happens less than quarterly. Methodology: BirdEye NPS data joined with self-reported MD engagement frequency, n=58 clinics (sampled from regions where engagement could be reliably documented). The +18 ppt is the headline number; the within-region effect (eliminating region-level confounders) is +14 ppt. Implication: clinical-leadership engagement is a quantitatively-significant client-experience driver, not just an HR-cultural concern. This evidence was the strongest single argument for the [[2025-11-10-medical-director-dyad-rollout]] decision — the dyad protects the time RMDs need to actually be in clinics.
