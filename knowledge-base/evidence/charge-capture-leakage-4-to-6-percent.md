# Charge-capture leakage 4-6%

Random audit of 200 visits across 12 clinics in Q3 2025 found 4-6% missed charges, weighted toward dispensing-side (drugs, topicals, samples) and dental-grade coding. Methodology: audited charts compared to billed invoices, structured by category. Recoverable production at $240M TTM is $9.6-14.4M annualized. Industry-comparable vet networks report 3-7%; Pawline is mid-pack. Largest single contributor: dental grading mismatches ([[dental-grade-3-undercoded-by-22-percent]]). [[2026-02-18-clinical-protocol-v2-publish]] is expected to recover ~50% of the gap; [[2026-04-30-ai-clinical-scribe-vendor]] is the next 20-30%.
