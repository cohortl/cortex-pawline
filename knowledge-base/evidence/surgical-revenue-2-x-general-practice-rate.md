# Surgical revenue 2x GP per-hour rate

Per-hour surgical revenue at Tier A flagship clinics averages 2.0x the GP per-hour rate ($580/hour vs $290/hour). Across all surgical-active clinics, the multiple is 1.7-2.3x. Methodology: Cornerstone visit-time data joined with billing data, calculated by visit type and DVM time. The multiple is the headline argument for the surgical-suite capex case in flagship clinics — surgical capacity utilization is the largest leverage point on flagship-clinic margin. Reinforces the [[hub-and-spoke-clinic-model]] economic logic and the build-vs-buy modeling for [[2026-03-30-emergency-clinic-line-of-service]] (ER procedures share many of the same per-hour-revenue characteristics as surgical procedures).
