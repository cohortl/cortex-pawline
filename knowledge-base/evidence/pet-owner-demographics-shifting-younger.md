# Pet-owner demographics shifting younger

Pawline's new-client acquisition mix in Q4 2025 was 41% millennial-aged (28-43 in 2026), up from 32% in 2023. Gen Z (under 28) was 14%, up from 7%. Boomer share (60+) was 19%, down from 26%. Methodology: Salesforce client demographic data joined with Cornerstone first-visit dates, n=14,800 new clients. Confidence is medium because demographic data isn't always cleanly captured (estimated from contact info + behavior; ~12% missing). Implications: predictable pricing models like [[2026-04-12-fixed-fee-pricing-pilot]], digital-first communication channels (text reminders, online booking), and pet insurance partnerships ([[2026-04-29-pet-insurance-partnership]]) all weight more heavily in our acquisition strategy if this shift continues.
