# Telehealth pilot 240% YoY growth

Telehealth visits across the 6 pilot clinics grew 240% from Q1 2025 (pre-launch) to Q1 2026. Off a small base (188 visits → 640 visits in Q1) so the percentage exaggerates the absolute movement, but the trajectory is durable across all 6 clinics with no flatness. Of the Q1 2026 visits, ~28% would have otherwise been in-clinic (cannibalization), ~72% net new. Average revenue per telehealth visit: $112 (vs in-clinic GP average $312). Lower revenue per visit but also lower DVM time cost — preliminary unit-economics modeling suggests telehealth is margin-positive at current volume. Drives the case for pilot extension decision in Q3 2026.
