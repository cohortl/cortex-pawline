# Equity participation program

Pawline's restricted-stock-unit program for tenured DVMs and operations leaders. Designed to give clinical and operational leaders a real stake in platform-level outcomes — not just clinic-level production. Owned by [[lisa-rodriguez]].

## Eligibility

- **DVMs**: Senior Associate level or above, ≥18 months tenure
- **Practice managers**: ≥24 months tenure, performance-rating "exceeds" or above
- **RMDs / RODs**: at appointment
- **Corporate roles**: Director-level and above, at hire (with 12-month cliff before any vest)
- **Acquired-clinic owners**: through [[seller-rollover-equity]] mechanism (different program structure)

## Grant sizing

Tied to role, region, and at-grant valuation:

| Tier | Initial grant range | Annual refresh range |
|------|---------------------|----------------------|
| Senior Associate DVM | $35K-$60K | $10K-$20K |
| Medical Director | $90K-$150K | $30K-$50K |
| Practice Manager | $40K-$70K | $15K-$25K |
| RMD / ROD | $200K-$350K | $75K-$125K |
| Director (corp) | $150K-$300K | $50K-$100K |
| VP / SVP | $400K-$700K | $150K-$250K |

All sizes are at the most recent 409A valuation; grants are subject to board sign-off above the VP level.

## Vesting

Per [[equity-vesting-policy]]:
- **Cliff**: 12 months for new-hire grants, no cliff for refreshers
- **Schedule**: 4-year quarterly thereafter
- **Acceleration**: pro-rata on death/disability, full on Pawline change of control
- **Forfeiture**: full forfeiture on for-cause termination; pro-rata vest preserved on resignation in good standing

## Take-up rate

[[equity-program-take-up-rate-78-percent]] — 78% of eligible DVMs accepted equity grants in 2025. The 22% who declined cited a mix of: liquidity preference (cash), tax planning, near-term retirement plans, or skepticism about Pawline's hold-period exit timing.

## Why this is hard at scale

- Communicating equity-grant value in concrete terms — "what does $90K of restricted stock mean if we're held privately for 5 more years?" — requires education most DVMs don't get from their 1099 background
- Managing the optics of grant size differences across regions
- Tax withholding at vest events (especially for newly-promoted MDs) requires HR + payroll coordination

## Why it matters strategically

- Aligns DVM and ops leader incentives with platform-level outcomes (the alternative — clinic-only ProSal incentives — produces clinic-level optimization that can hurt the network)
- Becomes the language we use during recruiting against larger consolidators
- Is the closest mechanism we have to genuine cultural ownership beyond clinical pride
