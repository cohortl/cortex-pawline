# Dr Amelia Foster — Independent Director

DVM, retired Banfield Pet Hospital regional medical director. Joined Pawline's board 2023 (in conjunction with the second Larkspur round) as the founding independent director. Brings 25 years of corporate-veterinary scaling experience.

## Role

- Voting director, independent
- Chairs the (informal) Clinical Quality committee — meets quarterly with [[dr-elena-vasquez]] and the RMDs
- Mentor to [[dr-sarah-okafor]] on board governance
- Brings the largest-rollup operating perspective on the board

## Why she's on the board

Sarah recruited her specifically for the Banfield experience. Pawline's clinical-cultural integrity question — how do you scale clinical quality past 50, 100, 200 clinics — is the question Amelia spent 15 years inside Banfield trying to answer. She's seen what works and what gets lost.

## Position on open decisions

- Strong proponent of the [[medical-director-dyad]] model — pushed for it during the Q4 2025 board
- Cautious on the [[2026-04-30-ai-clinical-scribe-vendor]] — wants to see workflow-impact evidence, not vendor demos
- Supportive of the [[2026-01-22-q3-ma-pause]] — has lived through M&A indigestion at Banfield's scale
- Skeptical of [[2026-03-30-emergency-clinic-line-of-service]] going greenfield; thinks acquisition is the right path

## Cadence

- Quarterly board
- Quarterly Clinical Quality committee
- Monthly informal touch with Sarah
