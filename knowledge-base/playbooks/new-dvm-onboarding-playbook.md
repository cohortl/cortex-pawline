# New DVM Onboarding Playbook

How Pawline onboards a new DVM in their first 90 days. Owned by the CPO with the regional RMD as accountable partner. Applied to every new DVM hire — new-grad through experienced lateral hire — with role-appropriate adjustments.

## The 90-day arc

- **Days 1-7**: orientation
- **Days 8-30**: paired mentorship
- **Days 31-60**: full schedule with check-ins
- **Days 61-90**: independent steady-state with quarterly review setup

## Day 1-7 — Orientation

- **Day 1**: HR orientation, IT setup, badges, tour of clinic + nearest flagship
- **Day 2-3**: Clinical orientation — [[clinical-standards-manual-v2]] walk-through, [[anesthesia-tier-policy]], [[dental-grading-system]], [[triage-acuity-levels]]
- **Day 4-5**: PIMS orientation (Cornerstone-specific) — full clinical workflow walk-through
- **Day 6-7**: Clinic-specific orientation — practice manager intro, technician team, scheduling philosophy, key clients

## Day 8-30 — Paired mentorship

- Paired with a senior associate or Medical Director within the same clinic
- Mentor reviews charts daily for first 2 weeks, weekly for next 2 weeks
- Schedule built around mentor availability — overlapping shifts where possible
- Weekly 30-minute check-in: clinical questions, operational questions, "what's surprising you"

## Day 31-60 — Full schedule with check-ins

- Independent caseload at full schedule
- Mentor reviews flagged cases weekly
- RMD touch monthly (or via the regional clinical-rounds visit)
- Mid-point: 45-day comp review (no changes; just confirming the agreement)

## Day 61-90 — Independent steady-state

- 90-day stay-interview ([[retention-model]]) — structured conversation with practice manager + RMD
- 90-day comp / production review (if ProSal — production share has begun)
- Clinic-quality-review check-ins integrated into normal cadence

## What's different for new-grads

- Extended mentorship (6 months instead of 1)
- Slower production ramp (no production share for first 3 months; salary-only)
- Specialty-rotation eligibility delayed to 12 months
- Equity grant eligibility at 18 months instead of standard 24

## What's different for laterals from acquired clinics

- DVMs joining via acquisition use this playbook on a 60-day cadence (compressed because they're already in the clinical workflow they know)
- Mentor pairing optional but offered

## Common pitfalls

- **Skipping mentor pairing in busy regions** — the highest-leverage retention intervention; non-negotiable even when staffing is tight
- **Comp surprises at 45-day review** — solved by clear comp conversation at offer
- **Tier-A flagship overload on day 1** — solved by orienting at Tier B clinic if hired into a Tier A flagship cluster
