# Equity Vesting Policy

Pawline's policy on how equity grants vest, accelerate, forfeit, or claw back. Applies to all grants under [[equity-participation-program]] + [[seller-rollover-equity]]. Owner: CPO + CFO (jointly). Audience: anyone holding or eligible for Pawline equity.

## Standard schedule

### New-hire grants

- **12-month cliff** before any vesting
- **Quarterly vesting thereafter** for 36 additional months
- **Total**: 48 months (4 years) to fully vest

### Refresh / annual grants

- **No cliff** (already at the company)
- **Quarterly vesting** for 48 months
- **Total**: 48 months

### Promotion grants

- **No cliff**
- **Quarterly vesting** for 48 months
- **Total**: 48 months

### Seller rollover equity

- **No cliff** (rollover is part of acquisition consideration)
- **Quarterly vesting** for 48 months
- **Total**: 48 months
- See [[seller-rollover-equity]] for additional details

## Acceleration triggers

### Full acceleration

- Pawline change of control (sale, IPO, merger > 50% change of ownership)
- Death of grantee
- Permanent disability of grantee

### Pro-rata acceleration

- Resignation in good standing — vested portion preserved; unvested forfeited
- Reduction in force — vested portion preserved + 6 months additional vest
- Termination without cause — same as RIF

## Forfeiture triggers

### Full forfeiture

- Termination for cause
- Material breach of employment / advisor agreement
- Resignation during a notice period of misconduct investigation

### Partial forfeiture

- Voluntary resignation: unvested forfeited; vested preserved
- See clawback section for vested-units recovery in specific circumstances

## Clawback

- **Material misconduct discovered post-departure**: vested portion subject to clawback per the original grant agreement
- **Restatement of financial results**: clawback of vested grants tied to materially misstated metrics, per Sarbanes-Oxley-equivalent provisions in the grant agreement
- **Non-compete violation**: clawback per the non-compete language in the grant agreement (state-specific enforceability varies)

## Tax planning

- Vesting events trigger taxable income at the then-current 409A valuation
- Pawline does not provide tax advice; grantees should consult their own tax advisors
- HR provides tax-event timing notices 90 days before each vest event

## How this policy interacts with the equity program

- This policy is the legal framework
- [[equity-participation-program]] is the operational program (eligibility, sizing, communication)
- Both must be read together to understand a grantee's full picture
